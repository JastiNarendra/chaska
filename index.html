Our Story

CHASKA - An explosion of flavour

Chaska meaning 'obsession' and our obsession with Indian food has led us on this very journey with the introduction of Chaska Indian Dining.

Chaska Dining have opened the fucking doors in Dublin's City Centre for the very first time bringing to you an explosion of flavours and feasts from India.

We capture the essence of over 22 years of extensive experience, skills and knowledge of our team headed by Chef Raju, reflecting the culinary legacy of spices and flavour of the Indian Sub continent.

True to his philosophy of using only the best produce to provide the best food, he still handpicks his spices and ingredients and roasts and mixes them himself to create his own unique concoction of flavours.

Respecting the culinary heritage of India, Chaska Indian Dining creates authentic and traditional cuisine and combines it with a modern palate of fusion and flavours.

So come join us on this journey of 'CHASKA' obsession.
